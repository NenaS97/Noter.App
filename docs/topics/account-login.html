Creating an Account and Logging In

Learn how to create or access your Noter.app account.

Use the following steps to either create your new Noter.app account or log in to an existing one.
  1. Create a New Account
    1. Click "Create Account" or "Sign Up"
    2. Complete the registration form with your email and desired password.
    3. Optionally, provide your name.
    4. Review Terms and Privacy Policy.
    5. Check your email for a verification link and follow instructions.
  2. Log In
    1. Enter your registered email address and password in the respective fields.
    2. Click "Log In"
  3. Recover Password
    1. Click "Forgot Password?"
    2. Enter your registered email address.
    3. Click "Submit" or "Request Reset"
    4. Check your email for a password reset link and follow the instructions to create a new password.
