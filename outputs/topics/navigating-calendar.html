Navigating the Calendar

Learn how to access and view the calendar feature in Noter.app.

This procedure explains how to open the calendar within Noter.app and view its basic layout.

  1. Open the Calendar
    1. In the Sidebar, locate and click the "Calendar" icon (refer to the Interface Overview for its location).
      - This action opens the calendar view in the main Note Editor Pane.
  2. Explore the Calendar Interface
    - The calendar displays the current month by default.
    - To view other months, use the navigation arrows ("<" and ">") located near the top of the calendar.
    - Click on a specific day to view any notes, tasks, or scheduled events for that day.
    - Some calendars may offer different views (day, week, month). Look for view options, represented by icons or tabs (e.g., "Day," "Week," "Month").
You are now viewing the Noter.app calendar. You can browse different months and days and view any associated notes or tasks.
